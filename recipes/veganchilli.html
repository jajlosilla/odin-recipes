Vegan Chilli

Ingredients

  • 2 canes of tomatoes
  • 2 canes of pinto beans
  • 1 cup of cooked white quinoa
  • 1 can of corn
  • oregano
  • smoked paprika
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cinnamon
  • 1 shot of expresso

    • Steps

    1. Add the caned tomatoes, pinto beans, expresso and spices to a large pot with 1 lt of water.
    2. Let everything zimmer and add the quinoa.
    3. Taste for salt and pepper. Serve.
