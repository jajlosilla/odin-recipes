Huevos rancheros

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 caned tomatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oregano
  • basil
  • 1 small onion
  • apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbl spooon of sugar

    • Steps

    1. In a pan fry the onions until soft.
    2. Cook the can tomatoes with the spices and a splash of vinegar.
    3. Once the sauce is cooked, put in the eggs and cook until soft.
