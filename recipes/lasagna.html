Aubergine Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 large aubergines
  • 1 kg cheese large
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oregano
  • basil
  • 500 gr shitake mushrooms
  • thime
  • 100ml white wine

    • Steps

    1. Cut aubergines, let sit in salted water for 30 min.
    2. Cut and cook mushrooms with sal, white wine and thime.
    3. Cook aubergines in the oven for 40min at 450c.
    4. Build the lasagna on a baking pan layering the cooked aubergine, mushrooms and cheese. In between each layer season well sith salt, pepper, oregano and basil. Finish up with a layer of cheese on top.
    5. Cook on the oven for 40 min at 180c.
