Tortilla de patatas

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes
  • 6 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Cut potatoes in 1cm thick pieces, onion in juliana and beat the eggs.
    2. In a nonstick pan with enought olive oil, cook the potatoes al dente.
    3. Take the potatoes out of the pan and then cook the onion until soft.
    4. Add the cooked potatoes and onion to the eggs add salt and to the pan again.
    5. Finish cooking the eggs, onion, potato mixture in the pan flipping the whole tortilla with a dish to cook both sides.
